FAQ

What is included?
One targeted lead research order with up to 50 business leads. The final deliverable is a clean spreadsheet based on your niche, location, and required data fields.
What fields can be included?
  • Company name
  • Website
  • Contact name if publicly available
  • Email if publicly available
  • LinkedIn page or profile if available
  • Location
What do you need from me?
  • Target niche or industry
  • Target location
  • Required number of leads, up to 50
  • Required fields
  • Optional example companies to calibrate the search
How many revisions are included?
One correction revision is included for issues such as duplicates, wrong location matches, or incorrect websites.
What is not included?
  • More than 50 leads in one order
  • Private or illegally collected contact data
  • Guaranteed personal emails where none are publicly available
  • Scraping behind logins or paywalls
