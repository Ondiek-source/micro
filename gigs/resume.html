FAQ

What is included?
One ATS-focused resume rewrite for one target role, plus one matching cover letter template. The service supports U.S., UK, and EU application formatting.
Which standard do you use?
  • U.S. resumes are kept to 1–2 pages on 8.5 × 11 inch letter-size layout.
  • If page 2 is used, it should be properly filled and not left half-empty.
  • Spacing is single or 1.15 with standard 1-inch margins.
  • Readable professional fonts and consistent heading hierarchy are used.
  • UK CVs are formatted in a clean, standard UK style.
  • EU applications can be prepared in the current Europass CV format.
Revisions in included Right?
Yes, up to 3 targeting revisions for the chosen job posting, plus 1 correctional revision for typos, dates, or small factual fixes.
What do you need from me?
  • Your current resume or work history notes
  • The target job posting or job description
  • Your preferred market: U.S., UK, or EU
  • Any achievements, metrics, or certifications you want highlighted
What is not included?
  • Unlimited rewrites
  • Executive or academic CVs beyond the stated scope
  • LinkedIn rewrite unless added separately
  • Career coaching or interview coaching
